Shepherd, Smith, Edwards & Kantas

Shepherd, Smith, Edwards & Kantas (SSEK) is a law firm that specializes in investment cases for investors who've lost their money due to lack of communication from the broker, fraud and simply taken advantaged of. When designing their website we wanted to highlight and emphasis their practice areas and add structure to their content so that it would be easier to access. Content was the primary focus for their website as they had years of news related articles that helped with Google rankings.
